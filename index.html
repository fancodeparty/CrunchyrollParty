Get Started with Crunchyroll Party: Your Complete Installation Guide

Crunchyroll Party saves the day if you love binge-watching anime with friends but distance keeps you apart. Hence, say goodbye to boredom and hello to endless fun as you stream anime, manga, and dorama with your loved ones using this fantastic extension. Furthermore, follow these simple steps to install and start using the Crunchyroll Party extension:

  • Download Crunchyroll Party Extension Ready to elevate your anime-watching experience? First, install the Crunchyroll extension on your device. Click here for a free installation, or head to the official Chrome Web Store and search for the Crunchyroll extension. You’ll need either Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome to use the Crunchyroll Party Chrome extension. This versatile extension works on Windows, macOS, Chromebooks, PCs, and laptops. Then, start enjoying seamless anime streaming with your friends, no matter the distance!
  • Pin The Extension To Your Toolbar After installing the extension, remember to pin it to your toolbar for easy access. However, if you don't see the icon, then click the "puzzle" icon located in the upper right corner of your browser. From the dropdown menu, find the Crunchyroll extension and pin it to your toolbar for convenient, one-click access.
  • Visit Crunchyroll Once you’ve installed and pinned the Crunchyroll Party extension, navigate to the Crunchyroll website and log in to your account. Ensure you have your own Crunchyroll account, as you’ll need it to host or join a Crunchyroll Watch Party. Further, prepare to dive into anime with your friends, no matter the distance!
  • Start Your Favorite Anime or Any Video Find the anime you want to watch with your friends at your Crunchyroll Party and press play. Whether it's anime, manga, dorama, or any other series or movies, you can enjoy it with anyone, anywhere in the world. Hence, prepare for a unique and shared viewing adventure!
  • Host a Crunchyroll Party Ready to start your Crunchyroll Watch Party? Click on the extension icon pinned to your toolbar. A "CREATE A WATCH PARTY" window will pop up, showcasing a "START A PARTY" button. Then, click it, and you'll get an invitation link. Further, share this link with your friends and start enjoying your favorite anime together!
  • Share the Invitation Link With your Crunchyroll Party invitation link ready, it’s time to assemble your crew. Spread the word by sharing the link through social media, messaging apps, or email. Moreover, invite all your friends to join the watch party and get ready to enjoy anime together in perfect harmony. The more friends, the more fun!
  • Join a Crunchyroll Party Are you excited to join a Crunchyroll Watch Party and binge-watch your favorite anime with friends? It's easy! First, download and pin the extension. Then, log into your Crunchyroll account. Finally, click on the invitation link sent to you. Just like that, you're in! Get ready for an incredible Crunchyroll Party experience, enjoying anime with your friends no matter where you are.
